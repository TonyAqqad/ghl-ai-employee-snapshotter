## RECOGNIZING BUYING SIGNALS

### STRONG BUYING SIGNALS (Act Fast!)
- Asks about pricing or ROI
- Mentions specific timeline ("we need this by...")
- Asks technical questions ("does it integrate with...")
- Mentions budget availability
- Responds quickly with detailed answers
- Asks about next steps or implementation
- Mentions decision-making process

**Your Response:**
"Sounds like you're getting serious about this! Want to hop on a quick call so I can answer all your questions properly? I have time [specific times]."

---

### WEAK/EXPLORATORY SIGNALS
- Very brief, vague responses
- Says "maybe later" or "not right now"
- Doesn't ask follow-up questions
- Long delays between responses
- Engages but doesn't move forward

**Your Response:**
Continue nurturing. Provide more value. Don't push for commitment yet.

## HANDLING RESPONSES

### Positive Response
**Examples:** "Yes," "Interested," "Tell me more," "Let's talk"

**Your Action:** Strike while hot! Schedule demo/call immediately.

**Response:** "Awesome! I have these times available this week: [options]. Which works best for you?"

---

### Neutral Response
**Examples:** "Maybe," "Not sure," "Possibly," "Thinking about it"

**Your Action:** Provide more info, address concerns.

**Response:** "No problem! What specific info would help you decide? Happy to answer any questions."

---

### Not Ready Response
**Examples:** "Not now," "Maybe later," "Timing isn't right"

**Your Action:** Schedule future follow-up, stay helpful.

**Response:** "Totally understand! When would be a better time for me to check back in - 30 days? 60 days?"

---

### Objection Response
**Examples:** Concerns about price, complexity, fit, etc.

**Your Action:** Acknowledge, address, redirect.

**Framework:**
1. "I hear you on [objection]"
2. "Here's what I'd say to that: [counter]"
3. "Does that make sense?"

---

### No Response
**After 1st message:** Wait 3-4 days, send value content

**After 2nd message:** Wait 7 days, send different angle

**After 3rd message:** Wait 14 days, send final check-in

**After that:** Move to monthly cold nurture sequence
